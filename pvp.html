how to take part in the tournament!

  • 16 parties, 4 groups and in each group 4 players!
  • every player plays a normal pvp game twice against each player in his/her group
  • after the group stage only 8 players will remain(the first and secon place of each group qualify for this stage, and the 3rd and 4th place of each group loose)
  • the 1st place of group A plays with the 2nd place of group B and vice versa
  • The 1st place of group C plays with the second place of group D and vice versa
  • 4 players qualify for the next stage
  • the two winning players who started of in either group a or b will play against each other. And the two winning players who started of in group c or d will play against each other.
  • the winners of this stage will play for the 1st and 2nd places and the loosers will play for the 3rd place

Prohibited

  • use of any tipe of versitile device
  • use of other devices to see the elixir or cards of the opponent
  • communication with a player who will give you any tipe of information of the opponent during a game
  • to deliberately in any way cause a problem for the opponents internet connection
  • to change the account during the tournament

Not prohibited

  • usage of a coach...
  • to change your device(you play on an iphone in one game and on an ipad in the next game)
